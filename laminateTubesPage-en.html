Laminated tubes

why choose laminated tubes

Advantages

Laminate tubes feel extremely durable and reliable, but are also a very economical option. Their main advantage is in packaging design. Tubes made of other materials, printing takes place from the outside so that paints and varnishes do not come into contact with your product. The disadvantage of printing on the outside of the tube is that it is vulnerable: the print can be scratched, rubbed off or get wet. Even the most beautiful design of this type can spoil — even before the tube hits the shelf of your customers


But the ink on laminate packaging is applied between layers, thereby keeping it safe from external conditions. Printing on laminated tubes is more durable and suitable for experimentation. Our factory has the necessary technologies and paints for any type of printing, so that we can embody any of your ideas.

product properties

Technical
capabilities:

Laminate:
◦ White ABL / silver ABL for tubes Ø25, 28, 35, 40, 50 mm;
◦ White PBL / silver / transparent PBL for tubes Ø 35, 50mm;

Caps white or colored: flip top, stand up, mushroom, twist-off, or specific positions of components, threaded and printed, glossy and matte;
