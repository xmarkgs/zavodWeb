Polyethylene tubes

why choose polyethylene tubes

Advantages

Polyethylene tubes are much lighter than metal and glass. We use polyethylene to create tubes, as it has great strength and puncture resistance with thinner calibers, and is also the most environmentally friendly. Polyethylene has the lowest softening point of any major packaging plastics and hence lower processing energy costs. This is important because lower energy costs lead to a lower carbon footprint.


By using plastic tubes, you don't have to worry about damage caused by oxidation and other chemical reactions. Polyethylene is corrosion resistant. Such tubes are easy to clean, just a simple soap and water is enough.

product properties

Technical
capabilities:

Tuba type: Monolaye, Duolayer, Multilayer with the possibility of body and shoulder color;

Material: polyethylene, COEX, mixture of HDPE and LDP, GreenPE;

Diameters: 19, 25, 30, 35, 40, 50;

Cannula: for tubes Ø19mm and Ø25mm;

LipStick for tubes Ø19mm;

Caps: flip top, stand up, soft line, "mushroom" or specific positions of accessories, threaded and printed, glossy and matte;

Control of the first opening in the form of a membrane (ALU / MPET) or cellophane (sleeve);

Printing type:

Offset (up to 8 colors + varnish),

Flexo printing (up to 7 colors + varnish),

Silk screen printing (up to 6 colors + varnish);

Hot stamping (gold, silver, color, holographic)

Full body color of tuba, shoulder and cap is possible;
