Aluminum tubes

Why choose aluminum tubes

Advantages

Aluminum tubes have several advantages. First, they are environmentally friendly. Aluminum is 100% recyclable. It can be recycled an infinite number of times without losing quality. Aluminum handling, transportation and recycling uses less energy than alternative materials — which in turn means lower carbon dioxide emissions.


In addition to this, aluminum tubes are very durable, they do not transmit light, liquid, air and microorganisms. Tubes made from this material are ideal for a wide range of products in a variety of fields, including food, pharmaceuticals and cosmetics. At the same time, you have a wide variety of design options, from embossing and debossing to printing and various labels.
