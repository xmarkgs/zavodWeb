A little about us

The success of the Tube Factory is determined by the enthusiasm of our employees who provide quality service and product reliability. All employees, from janitors to the CEO, receive internal and external training and then certification.


Our specialists bring cross-industry experience and constantly invent solutions for more efficient and quality production. We nurture our specialists by providing them with resources to improve and maintain their qualifications, favorable working conditions, social activities and a reward system.


Career growth is not a buzz-word for us, but a reality. There are many stories of successful development, when a person started out as a designer and became a production manager.
